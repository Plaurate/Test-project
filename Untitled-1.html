Arctic monkeys

Arctic Monkeys are an English rock band formed in Sheffield in 2002. The group consists of lead singer Alex Turner, drummer Matt Helders, guitarist Jamie Cook and bassist Nick O'Malley. Former bassist Andy Nicholson left the band in 2006 shortly after their debut album was released.

Current members of the band

Alex Turner

Alexander David Turner is an English singer and musician. He is best known as the frontman and principal songwriter of the rock band Arctic Monkeys, with whom he has released seven studio albums. He has also recorded with his side project involving Miles Kane, as the Last Shadow Puppets and also as a solo artist.

Jamie Cook

Jamie Robert Cook born on 8th of July 1985 is an English guitarist and songwriter. He is best known as the guitarist and a founding member of the indie rock band Arctic Monkeys, with whom he has recorded seven studio albums.

Matt Helders

Matthew Helders is an English drummer, vocalist and songwriter. He is best known as a founding member and the drummer of indie rock band Arctic Monkeys, with whom he has recorded seven studio albums.

Nicholas O'Malley

Nicholas O'Malley became bassist of the British rock band the Arctic Monkeys. He began playing with the group in 2006, filling in for original Arctic Monkeys bass guitarist Andy Nicholson.
